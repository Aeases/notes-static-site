### Definition
>[!cite] Cycle
>A Cycle is a subtype of a [[Walk#Closed Walk|Closed Walk]] that **does not repeat** any [[Edge]]s or [[Vertex|Verticies]], except for the **start and finish**. 
>It is identical to a [[Path#Closed Path|Closed Path]].
