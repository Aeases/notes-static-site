>[!cite] Eulers Formula
>Eulers formula is always true a graph is a valid [[Planar Graph]].

$$v+f-e=2$$
Where:
$v$ = [[Vertex|Verticies]]
$f$ = [[Face]]s
$e$ = [[Edge]]s
