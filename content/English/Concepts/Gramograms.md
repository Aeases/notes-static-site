### Definition
>[!cite] Gramograms
>Gramograms are sequences of letters that when said together, sound like a word (e.g. QT)

#### Examples of Gramograms
| Letters | Word    |
| ------- | ------- |
| IC      | Icey    |
| XS      | Excess  |
| DK      | Decay   |
| MLE     | Emily   |
| QT      | Cutie   |
| JQZ     | Jacuzzi |
