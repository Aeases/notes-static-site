>[!cite] Appeals
>Appeals are what a specific text is trying to use to *appeal* to something *in* their reader, e.g. appeal to their sense of tradition; self-interest; fear; symphaty etcetera.

### Types of Appeals
- [[Logos]] (Logic)
- [[Pathos]] (Emotion)
- [[Ethos]] ((Credibility / Authority)
- [[Kairos]] ("Time of life")
